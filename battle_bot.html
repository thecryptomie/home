Algorillas Battle Bot

TLDR

The Battle Bot, developed by the Algorillas, is a discord bot that utilizes ARC69 traits to perform weighted-RNG battles between any two ARC69 NFTs.

Multicollection Support

For each collection, the rarity score of each NFT is computed using its ARC69 traits. Since each collection has their own distribution of traits, we normalize the rarity scores using min/max normalization to obtain values between 0 and 1. Next, we multiplty those by an arbitrary 1000 to get values between 0 and 1000.

The Functionality

Probabilities

For each matchup, the normalized rarity scores are used to compute the chances of winning for each NFT.
If RSN is the rarity score of player N, then the odds for each player are given by,
Player 1: RS1/(RS1 + RS2)
Player 2: RS2/(RS1 + RS2)

Example

AGA405 vs AGA301
AGA405 odds: 100*(557.345/(1000.000 + 557.345)) = 35.79%
AGA301 odds: 100*(1000.000/(1000.000 + 557.345)) = 64.21%

Using the probabilities computed above, we then perform a random selection where the probabilities correspond to the weights in the random draw. In this matchup, AGA301 has a 64.21% of being drawn.

Each battle consists of 5 draws, the NFT chosen 3 times is the winner and the battle statistics are recorded. In the battle below, AGA301 (orange space suit) defeated AGA405 (laser eyes) with a final score of 3 to 1.

Battle Bot Commands

The Algorillas Battle Bot has been implemented using discord slash commands. When typing in a slash command, discord will show you a list of suggested options. Make sure to click on the option associated with the Battle Bot and not one of the other bots in our discord. The video below demonstrates how to identify the battle bot registration command amongst the slash commands suggested by discord.

Command Description
/register Register your wallet address or NFD with the battle bot
/wallet_info Show the list AGAs held by the wallet registered by the user. Listed AGA are excluded.
/add Add an AGA to the battle list. This makes the AGA available for battle and signs you up for any future Battle Weeks.
/remove Remove an AGA from your battle list. You can only battle with AGAs that are in your wallet
/my_entries Show all of the AGAs added to the battle list by the user. If you list an AGA you have entered, it will be removed from this list.
/entrants Show the list of AGAs that are available for battling.
/weekly_battles Show the list of weekly battles you have been assigned. After a battle is completed, the corresponding entry will be shown with strikethrough text.
/battle Battle an available AGA using the battle bot.. To conduct a weekly battle, you need to pass the weekly argument like you see below. If not, it will only count towards your all time record.
/my_stats List your all time battle stats and your weekly battle stats.
