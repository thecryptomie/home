Algorillas Battle Week

Battle Week #2 is live!
Registration closes on September 30th at midnight and battles begin October 1st.
Join the discord for more info: Algorillas Discord

TLDR

Battle Week is a week-long contest where Algorilla Army holders go head-to-head for prizes. The battle winners will be determined by the battle bot. Each participant will be assigned a randomly generated list of 25 battles (this number may change) and have 7 days to complete them. Each battle week, a series of traits will be chosen and given a boost to enhance their overall rarity score and increase their odds of winning a battle. Prizes will be given out each Battle Week (ALGO, NFTs, WL spots, and who knows what else!)

Battle Week Phases

Registration

Everyone who wants to participate registers with the battle_bot in the #battles channel of the algorillas discord.

Battling

After registration closes, each registered user is assigned a battle list that they must complete throughout the week.

Determining the Winner

At the end of the week, the user with the highest record is declared the winner and takes the first place prize.

Battle Bot Functionality

Probabilities

For each matchup, the rarity scores are used to compute the chances of winning for each NFT.
If RSN is the rarity score of player N, then the odds for each player are given by,
Player 1: RS1/(RS1 + RS2)
Player 2: RS2/(RS1 + RS2)

Example

AGA405 vs AGA301
AGA405 odds: 100*(316.33/(469.11 + 316.33)) = 40.27%
AGA301 odds: 100*(469.11/(469.11 + 316.33)) = 59.73%

Using the probabilities computed above, we then perform as random selection where the probabilities correspond to the weights in the random draw. In this matchup, AGA301 has a 59.73% of being drawn.

Each battle consists of 5 draws, the NFT chosen 3 times is the winner and the battle statistics are recorded. In the battle below, AGA301 (orange space suit) defeated AGA405 (laser eyes) with a final score of 3 to 1.

Battle Bot Commands

Command Description
/register Register your wallet address or NFD with the battle bot
/wallet_info Show the list AGAs held by the wallet registered by the user. Listed AGA are excluded.
/add Add an AGA to the battle list. This makes the AGA available for battle and signs you up for any future Battle Weeks.
/remove Remove an AGA from your battle list. You can only battle with AGAs that are in your wallet
/my_entries Show all of the AGAs added to the battle list by the user. If you list an AGA you have entered, it will be removed from this list.
/entrants Show the list of AGAs that are available for battling.
/weekly_battles Show the list of weekly battles you have been assigned. After a battle is completed, the corresponding entry will be shown with strikethrough text.
/battle Battle an available AGA using the battle bot.. To conduct a weekly battle, you need to pass the weekly argument like you see below. If not, it will only count towards your all time record.
/my_stats List your all time battle stats and your weekly battle stats.
