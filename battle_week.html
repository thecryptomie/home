Algorillas Battle Week

Battle Week is currently not active.

TLDR

Battle Week is a week-long contest where Algorillas holders go head-to-head for prizes. The battle winners will be determined by the battle bot. Each participant will be assigned a randomly generated list of 25 battles (this number may change) and have 7 days to complete them. Prizes will be given out each Battle Week for 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place.

Battle Week Phases

Phase 1: Registration

Everyone who wants to participate registers with the battle_bot in the #battles channel of the algorillas discord.

Phase 2: Battling

After registration closes, each registered user is assigned a battle list that they must complete throughout the week. The battle list is randomly generated for each user using the full list of entrants from all participating collections.

Phase 3: Tallying the Results

At the end of the week, the battle records of all competitors are compared to determine 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place.
